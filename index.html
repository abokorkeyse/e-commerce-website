About Dookh

Dookh waa goob eCommerce casri ah oo kuu fududeyneysa inaad si sahlan u hesho alaabo tayo sare leh oo la jaanqaadaya dookhaaga. Waxaan aaminsanahay in macaamiishu ay mudan yihiin adeeg hufan, naqshad nadiif ah, iyo khibrad wax iibsasho oo sahlan.

Waxaan diiradda saarnaa:

  • Soo bandhigidda alaabo tayo leh
  • Bixinta adeeg degdeg ah oo la isku halleyn karo
  • Naqshad nadiif ah (minimalist) oo sahlan in la dhex maro

Dookh waa meesha dookhaaga laga dhigo mid dhab ah.
